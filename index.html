Got Questions?

Frequently asked

How quickly will a driver arrive?▼
We guarantee a driver at your location within 30 minutes anywhere in Raipur, Bhilai, Durg, or Bilaspur. In most cases drivers arrive even faster — often 15–20 minutes.
Is the service available at night?▼
Absolutely. DriveBuddy operates 24 hours, 7 days a week including all public holidays. Our night safety driver service (8 PM – 6 AM) is popular with women, families, and late-night office commuters.
Difference between Local and Outstation?▼
Local (₹1000–1200) covers full-day city driving within Raipur, Bhilai, Durg or Bilaspur. Outstation (₹1200–1500) is for travel beyond city limits — call 9111473929 for a custom quote.
What if I need to extend my booking?▼
You can extend directly with the driver or by calling us. Extension charges are per-hour. The driver stays with you until the job is done — no rushing.
How are drivers verified?▼
Every driver completes a 4-stage verification: Government ID, police background check, breathalyzer before every shift, and 5+ years professional driving experience. We conduct periodic re-verification.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfer. Simple and transparent — zero hidden charges whatsoever.
Can I book for hospital emergencies?▼
Yes — medical and emergency bookings are our absolute priority. Call 9111473929 immediately. We dispatch the nearest available driver as fast as possible, any hour of day or night.
How does the online booking form work?▼
Fill in your details and submit — your booking request is saved directly to our system. We will call you within minutes to confirm your driver.
